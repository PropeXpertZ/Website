Home Loan Documents Checklist

Keep these documents handy for faster loan processing.

For Salaried Individuals

  • Filled Application Form with Photograph
  • Identity Proof (PAN / Passport / Voter ID / Aadhaar)
  • Residence Proof (Utility Bill / Passport / Aadhaar)
  • Latest 3 Months Salary Slips
  • Form 16 for last 2 years
  • Bank Statement for last 6 months (Salary Account)
  • Processing Fee Cheque

For Self-Employed (Business)

  • Filled Application Form with Photograph
  • Identity & Residence Proof
  • Business Proof (GST Registration / Trade License)
  • ITR for last 3 years with Computation of Income
  • Balance Sheet & P&L Account (CA Certified)
  • Bank Statement for last 12 months (Current & Savings)

Property Documents

  • Sale Agreement / Sale Deed
  • NOC from Society / Builder
  • Occupancy Certificate (for ready move)
  • Approved Building Plan
  • Property Tax Receipts
