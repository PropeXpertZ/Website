Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your personal information.

Effective Date: January 10, 2026  |  Last Updated: January 10, 2026

Executive Summary: This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and share it, the safeguards we apply, and what choices and rights you have regarding your personal data.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the PropXpertz platform, including website visitors, registered users, buyers, sellers, tenants, landlords, and real estate professionals who interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including your full name, email address, telephone number, location preferences, property interests, inquiry details, appointment requests, and communications you send to us through forms, email, phone, or chat.

We also automatically collect technical and usage information such as your IP address, browser type and version, device identifiers, operating system, pages viewed, links clicked, referral URLs, timestamps, session duration, and interaction patterns.

We use cookies, pixels, tags, and similar tracking technologies to remember preferences, enable site functionality, analyze performance, personalize content, and measure marketing effectiveness.

2. Purposes of Collection

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, respond to your inquiries, connect you with relevant real estate professionals, manage user accounts, personalize listings and recommendations, conduct internal analytics and research, prevent fraud and abuse, communicate with you about services, updates, or promotions (with consent), and comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on your consent where required, our contractual obligations to provide requested services, our legitimate interests in improving, securing, and operating the platform, and our obligation to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share personal information with real estate agents, brokers, developers, property owners, and service providers in order to fulfill your requests or facilitate transactions.

We may also share information with trusted technology vendors such as hosting providers, analytics services, customer relationship management (CRM) systems, communication platforms, and payment processors.

We may disclose information when required by law, regulation, court order, or governmental request. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

5. International Transfers

Your personal data may be processed or stored outside your country of residence. When this occurs, we implement appropriate safeguards such as contractual protections or other legal mechanisms to ensure adequate protection.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal, accounting, or regulatory requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to your personal data, correct inaccurate information, request deletion, restrict or object to certain processing activities, and withdraw consent where applicable.

You may also have the right to opt out of certain communications or data uses. Requests can be submitted by contacting us using the details below.

8. Cookies and Tracking

You can control cookies through your browser settings, delete existing cookies, or block future cookies. Some site features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

9. Security Measures

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards such as encryption, secure servers, access controls, monitoring, and employee training to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

10. Children’s Privacy

The PropXpertz platform is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will promptly delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or services. The updated version will be posted with a revised effective date.
